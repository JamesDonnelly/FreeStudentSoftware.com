Here, what's all this then?

If you're a student then you're in luck! A few rather awesome companies allow people in education to get their hands on amazing pieces of software for free! Free Student Software.com is an attempt to list them all in one place.

Click on the boxes below to visit the distributor's website.

Autodesk Save Thousands

Autodesk's Education Community offers a large amount of free engineering and design software packages to students and educators. These include the likes of AutoCAD and 3ds Max along with their Ultimate Product Design, Building Design and Entertainment Creation Suites.

Engineering Design Animation 3d CAD

Microsoft Save Thousands

DreamSpark and OnTheHub are Microsoft's portals for free tools for anyone in education. Depending on what your institution has signed up for, you can get the full Visual Studio, SQL Server, some Office programs and even various Windows operating systems along with a wide range of other powerful packages for no cost whatsoever!

Programming Design Robotics Office Operating Systems

Intel Save Thousands!

Intel's Free Tools for Students programme allows anyone with a recognised student email address to access its Parallel Studio XE, System Studio and Video Pro Analyzer software absolutely free of charge.

Programming Audio Video

GitHub Save Hundreds!

GitHub have teamed up with loads of different companies to offer their Student Developer Pack. Not only do you get access to the likes of Visual Studio, Unreal Engine, Atom and SendGrid, you also get all the benefits of a GitHub Micro account and can also claim a free .me domain.

Programming Websites The Cloud

JetBrains Save Hundreds!

JetBrains's Free For Students: Professional Develoer Tools does what it says on the tin. It offers all of its incredibly popular development packages for free to students.

Programming Web Development Mobile Development

Axure Save Hundreds!

Axure's popular wireframing, prototyping and documentation tool Axure RP Pro is available for free through their Free Licenses for Students and Teachers initiative.

Wireframing Prototyping Documentation
